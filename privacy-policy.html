Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Ambitionz Publisher · @nicolasaltamirano06

Ambitionz Publisher ("the Application") is a private, personal-use tool used solely by its owner to automate the scheduling and publishing of video content to social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook.

1. Information We Collect

The Application accesses your social media accounts only through official platform APIs using OAuth tokens that you personally authorize. No personal data from third-party users is collected, stored, or processed.

The Application stores locally:

  • Your platform access tokens (stored in environment variables on your own device)
  • Video content and captions you create and schedule
  • Scheduling metadata (post times, platform targets)

2. How We Use the Information

Access tokens are used exclusively to publish and schedule content on your own accounts. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party other than the platforms you explicitly authorize (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook).

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your personal device or in your private GitHub repository. No third-party servers beyond the official platform APIs are involved.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application connects to the following services on your behalf:

  • TikTok Content Posting API — to upload and publish videos
  • Instagram Graph API — to publish Reels
  • YouTube Data API — to upload and schedule videos
  • Meta Graph API — to publish Facebook videos
  • Cloudinary — to temporarily host video files for API access
  • Groq API — to generate captions via AI (no video data is sent)

5. Data Retention

The Application does not retain platform access tokens beyond your local device. You can revoke access at any time through each platform's security settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. No data from minors is knowingly collected.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
